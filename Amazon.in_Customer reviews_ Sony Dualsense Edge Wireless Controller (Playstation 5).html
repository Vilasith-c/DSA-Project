    • Aakshat Paandey
      3.0 out of 5 stars PS5 DualSense Edge Controller
      Reviewed in India on 19 September 2024
      Verified Purchase
      I recently purchased the PS5 DualSense Edge controller, and as a user experience designer and avid gamer, I was eager to see how it compared to the original DualSense. Heres my take!

      Pros:

      Customization: The ability to remap buttons and adjust sensitivity settings is fantastic. It really allows you to tailor the controller to your play style, making the gaming experience feel more personal.
      Ergonomics: The design is a step upvery comfortable to hold, even during long sessions. I noticed less hand fatigue, which is a huge plus.
      Immersive Feedback: The improved tactile feedback and adaptive triggers enhance immersion. Every action feels more impactful, adding depth to gameplay.
      Cons:

      Price: At 18,000, this controller is nearly 3x the cost of the original DualSense. While I appreciate the enhancements, I honestly believe it should be priced closer to 10,000. The high cost makes it feel a bit exclusive, and I think many gamers might be hesitant to invest at this price point.
      Overall, the DualSense Edge offers some great improvements, but the steep price tag holds it back from being a must-have. If youre considering the upgrade, weigh the benefits against your budget.

      Hope this helps fellow gamers make an informed decision!
